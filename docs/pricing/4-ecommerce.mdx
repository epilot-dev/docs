Key features:

- [Journeys & Journey Builder](/docs/journeys/journey-builder)

Build multi-step journeys for selling complex products. Submissions collect customer data and create orders automatically.

Share the journey URL in marketing campaigns, customize branding with the [Design Builder](/docs/journeys/design-builder), and [embed journeys](/docs/journeys/journey-builder#embedding-journeys) on your website.
