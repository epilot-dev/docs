The product catalog manages all products, prices, and tax rates. It serves as the foundation for journeys, orders, and pricing features.

## Feature Highlights {#feature-highlights}

All catalog entities support:

- **Relations** -- quick access to related entities
- **Custom fields** via the Entity Builder
- **Workflows** -- automate processes around products and prices
- **Search and filter** -- powerful query options
- **Multi-currency** support
- **Notes and attachments**
- **Metadata view** -- full read/write access to underlying entity data
